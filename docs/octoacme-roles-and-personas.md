# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Product Owner   <!-- NEW -->
### Role Summary
The Product Owner is responsible for defining the product vision and acting as the bridge between stakeholders and the delivery team.

### Responsibilities
- Define and prioritize product features and backlog items
- Represent stakeholders in delivery planning
- Clarify requirements and acceptance criteria with the team
- Make final calls on scope trade-offs for delivery

### Goals
- Ensure the product delivers maximum value within constraints
- Maintain continual alignment with business needs
- Shorten feedback loops between users and dev teams

### Interactions
Works closely with Project Manager, Product Manager, Developers, and Stakeholders.

---

## Quality Assurance Lead   <!-- NEW -->
### Role Summary
The Quality Assurance Lead ensures quality checks and standards are implemented across all delivery phases.

### Responsibilities
- Oversee creation and execution of test plans and cases
- Define and monitor quality gates prior to release
- Report test results and issues for remediation

### Goals
- Ensure defect-free releases
- Maintain traceability of requirements to tests
- Foster a culture of quality and continuous improvement

### Interactions
Collaborates with Developers, Product Owner, Project Manager, and Technical Writer.

---

## Communications Specialist   <!-- NEW -->
### Role Summary
Responsible for crafting and delivering project updates internally and externally.

### Responsibilities
- Create communication plans for the project
- Draft and send periodic status updates
- Document key meeting decisions and changes

### Goals
- Ensure all stakeholders are informed and aligned
- Improve transparency and clarity in project progress

### Interactions
Coordinates with Project Manager, Product Owner, Stakeholders, and Business Analyst.

---

## Technical Writer   <!-- NEW -->
### Role Summary
Document process, outcomes, and artifacts to drive understanding and clarity.

### Responsibilities
- Prepare and update project management and technical documentation
- Ensure artifacts are accessible and understandable to all audiences
- Track document changes and versioning

### Goals
- Minimize ambiguity in technical and process guidance
- Deliver clear documentation to support onboarding and delivery

### Interactions
Works with Developers, QA Lead, Project Manager, and Communications Specialist.

---

## Business Analyst   <!-- NEW -->
### Role Summary
Bridge between requirements gathering and solution design.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct stakeholder interviews and collect requirements
- Analyze and clarify business needs
- Create actionable work items and acceptance criteria

### Goals
- Reduce gap between stakeholder intent and deliverable solutions
- Validate delivered features against original requirements

### Interactions
Works closely with Product Owner, Project Manager, Communications Specialist, and Developers.
